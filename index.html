Privacy Policy for Chameleon VPN

Last updated: September 04, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Service and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Chameleon VPN does not collect or retain your personal data directly. However, we use third-party services, specifically AdMob for advertisements and VPNGate for VPN services. This Privacy Policy outlines how these services handle your information.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include information such as your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.

Use of Your Personal Data

Chameleon VPN does not store or retain any personal data. However, when you use the Service, third-party services may collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

AdMob

AdMob may collect information to serve personalized advertisements based on your usage patterns. This data helps in delivering relevant ads and measuring their effectiveness.

VPNGate

VPNGate logs certain information related to your use of their VPN services for security and abuse prevention purposes. This may include:

  • IP addresses (source and destination)
  • Connection timestamps (date, time, and duration)
  • VPN protocol details and connection metadata
  • Amount of data transferred

These logs are retained by VPNGate for at least three months and are used solely for monitoring abuse and ensuring network security. While Chameleon VPN does not have direct access to these logs, VPNGate may disclose them to authorities if legally required.

Logging Policy

For more information about VPNGate’s logging policy, please refer to their Anti-Abuse Policy.

Withdrawal of Consent

You have the right to withdraw your consent to the logging and processing of your personal data at any time. Upon withdrawing consent:

  • We will immediately disconnect you from the VPN service, and you will no longer be able to use the app.
  • Your data logs will no longer be collected or processed by us or VPNGate.
  • Requesting Data Deletion: If you withdraw consent, we will request that VPNGate delete any logs they have collected about your usage of the service. However, VPNGate retains logs for a minimum of two weeks, and logs might already be deleted or compressed to save disk space.

Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete or request that we assist in deleting the Personal Data that may have been collected about you by third-party services. However, please note that Chameleon VPN itself does not store or retain any personal data. You may update or delete your information at any time by following the procedures set by those third-party services.

Retention of Your Personal Data

Chameleon VPN does not retain any personal data; all user data is stored by third-party services such as VPNGate and AdMob. These services retain user data according to their own policies and for their required durations. For more information on how long these third-party services retain your data, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Security of Your Personal Data

The security of your Personal Data is important to us. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We do not disclose personal data directly; however, third-party services may disclose your personal data under the following circumstances:

  • Business Transactions: In the case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale involving those services.
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law or in response to valid legal requests related to those services.
  • Other Legal Requirements: To protect their rights, investigate wrongdoing, or ensure the personal safety of users.

Children’s Privacy

Chameleon VPN does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. However, third-party services, such as AdMob and VPNGate, may collect such information in accordance with their own privacy policies. If we become aware that any personal data collected from a child under the age of 13 has been obtained, we will take steps to delete that information. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce our policy by instructing their children never to provide personal data without their permission.

For more information on how AdMob and VPNGate handle children's data, please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • VPNGate Anti-Abuse Policy
