Logging Policy

Our app uses VPNGate to provide VPN services. VPNGate logs certain information for security and abuse prevention purposes, including:
- IP addresses (source and destination)

These logs are retained by VPNGate for at least three months and are used solely for monitoring abuse and ensuring network security.
